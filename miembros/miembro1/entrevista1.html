Get to know Alex Kapranos

Alex Kapranos, the lead vocalist and guitarist of Franz Ferdinand, talks about his music, inspirations, and experiences.

Alex Kapranos

Role: Lead Vocals, Guitar

Q: What inspired you to become a musician?

A: I’ve always been drawn to music from an early age. The idea of creating something that connects with others on an emotional level is something that really excites me. Growing up listening to bands like The Strokes and Talking Heads really shaped my musical tastes.

Q: What has been the most memorable moment in your career so far?

A: There have been so many moments, but playing at festivals and seeing the audience sing along to our songs is a feeling I can’t describe. It’s a surreal experience to see how far our music has come and how it resonates with people all over the world.

Q: Can you tell us about the process of writing songs for Franz Ferdinand?

A: It usually starts with an idea, whether it’s a riff, a melody, or a lyric. We all work together in the studio, bouncing ideas off each other, and that’s when the magic happens. The process is always collaborative, and everyone in the band contributes their own unique perspective.
