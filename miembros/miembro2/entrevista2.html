Get to Know Bob Hardy

Bob Hardy, the talented bassist of Franz Ferdinand, shares his thoughts on music, the band's journey, and more.

Bob Hardy

Role: Bass

1. How did you get started in music?

I started playing music at a young age. My family was very supportive of the arts, and I quickly fell in love with the bass guitar. It became an essential part of who I am today.

2. What's the most memorable moment you've had with Franz Ferdinand?

There have been many memorable moments, but one that stands out is playing in front of thousands of fans at Glastonbury. It felt like a surreal dream coming true.

3. How do you approach songwriting as a bassist?

I think of the bass as the foundation of the song. My approach is always to support the melody, lock in with the drums, and give space for the rest of the band to shine. It's about finding the groove.

4. What's your favorite Franz Ferdinand song to perform live?

“Take Me Out” will always be a crowd favorite, and it’s so much fun to play live. The energy from the crowd makes every performance a unique experience.

5. What’s next for you and Franz Ferdinand?

We’re always looking forward to the next adventure. There’s more music to come, and we can’t wait to hit the road again. We’re still passionate about creating new sounds and sharing them with our fans.
