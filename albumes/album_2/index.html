Franz Ferdinand

This is the second studio album by Scottish indie rock band Franz Ferdinand, released on September 28, 2005. Known for its indie rock and dance-punk sound, it includes hits like "Do You Want To" and "The Fallen". The album received critical acclaim and topped the UK Albums Chart.
